# Customer-Churn-Analysis
**📊 Customer Churn Analysis – SQL + Power BI**

**🎯 Project Objective**
To analyze customer subscription behavior and identify key drivers of churn using SQL and Power BI. The project focuses on quantifying revenue loss and identifying high-risk customer segments to support retention strategies.

**📁 Dataset**
Dataset Name: Customer Subscription Churn and Usage Patterns
Source: Kaggle
Purpose: Educational and portfolio demonstration
Records: 2800 customers

**Key Fields:**
-> Tenure Months
-> Usage Frequency
-> Support Tickets
-> Payment Failures
-> Plan Type
-> Churn

🛠 **Tools Used**
SQL – Data Cleaning & Aggregation
Power BI – Dashboard & Visualization
DAX – KPI Calculations & Risk Segmentation

📌 **Key KPIs Built**
-Total Customers
-Churn Rate %
-Revenue Lost Due to Churn
-Average Revenue Per User (ARPU)
-Revenue at Risk

🔎 **Key Insights**
-Customers in early tenure stage show highest churn.
-Low-usage customers churn significantly more.
-Higher support calls correlate with increased churn.
-Monthly subscription plans have higher churn rates.
-High-risk customer segment contributes significant revenue exposure.

**💡 Business Recommendations**

-Improve onboarding experience for new users.
-Launch engagement campaigns for low-usage customers.
-Enhance customer support resolution time.
-Promote annual subscription plans with incentives.
-Proactively target high-risk segment with retention offers.

**📈 Business Impact**

The analysis quantified revenue loss due to churn and identified behavioral patterns driving attrition, enabling targeted retention strategies.
